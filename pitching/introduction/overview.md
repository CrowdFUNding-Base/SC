## Donate. Evolve. Impact.

**CrowdFUNding** is a revolutionary fundraising layer built on Base that transforms the transactional act of giving into a gamified lifestyle. CrowdFUNding addresses the "Donor Death Spiral" where 81% of contributors exit after a single transaction and 12.66% of donation value is lost to banking fees.

By bridging the gap between high-velocity Web3 mechanics and Indonesia's status as the #1 most generous nation, the platform eliminates "donor fatigue" and replaces it with a "positive-sum game." Every contribution creates an immutable, evolving digital legacy, turning the "Generational Exit" of Gen Z donors into a lifetime of verifiable impact.

## Why Built on Base?

Base is the only ecosystem capable of supporting a high-frequency, low-margin consumer application that requires the speed and affordability of Web2 with the trustlessness of Web3:

- **Micro-Donation Viability**: Base's near-zero transaction fees allow the protocol to process donations as small as Rp 10,000 (~$0.65) without the fees eating the impactâ€”solving the "12.66% Financial Tax" problem.
- **Consumer Crypto Standard**: CrowdFUNding aligns perfectly with Base's mission to shift the narrative from speculative "PvP Casinos" to meaningful "PvE Communities," bringing the next billion users onchain through real-world utility.
- **Seamless Authentication**: The app offers dual authentication via **Google OAuth 2.0** (server-side) and **RainbowKit** (Web3 wallets), making the platform accessible to both Web2 and Web3 users with optional wallet-account linking.
