# 13. Competitive Landscape

CrowdFUNding disrupts the market by eliminating banking tolls to maximize social impact.

| **Transparency**      | "Trust me, bro" (PDFs) | Manual Audits                 | **Code is Law** (On-Chain)   |

## Our Advantages

1.  **Cost Leadership**: By leveraging Base L2, we cut the "Middleman Tax" by >50%.
2.  **Velocity**: Instant settlement is a game-changer for emergency relief (e.g., floods, earthquakes) where waiting 3 days for bank clearance is not an option.
3.  **Trust**: We replace opaque manual processes with immutable smart contracts, appealing deeply to the skepticism of Gen Z donors.
