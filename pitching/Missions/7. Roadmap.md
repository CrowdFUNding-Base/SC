# 7. Roadmap

**Phase 1: Hackathon & MVP (Current)**

- **Smart Contracts**: Deployment of `CampaignFactory`, `Campaign` logic, and `SwapRouter` integration on Base Sepolia.
- **Core Features**:
  - Create Campaign capability.
  - Donate with ETH/USDC (Auto-swap).
  - Donate with IDRX.
- **Frontend**: Basic dashboard for Campaigners and Donors using Next.js.
- **Security**: Implementation of `ReentrancyGuard` and standard OpenZeppelin access controls.

**Phase 2: Alpha Polish & Gamification**

- **Midtrans Integration**: robust backend service to handle Fiat -> IDRX minting/transfer.
- **Enhanced Gamification**: Launch of "Donor Tiers" and NFT minting upon milestone completion.
- **Leaderboards**: Global and per-campaign leaderboards to drive competition.

**Phase 3: Beta & Partnerships**

- **Audit**: Professional security audit of smart contracts.
- **Mainnet Launch**: Deploy to Base Mainnet.
- **Pilot Program**: Partner with 5-10 trusted local NGOs to launch the first verified campaigns.
- **Mobile Mini-App**: Development of a mobile-first interface optimized for Coinbase Wallet or social apps.

**Phase 4: Decentralized Governance**

- **DAO**: Transitioning platform verification to a community-voted DAO model.
- **Token**: Potential launch of a governance token (FUN) to reward active platform participants.
