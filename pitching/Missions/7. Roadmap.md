# 7. Roadmap (2026)

## Q1 2026: Foundation & Compliance

**"Building the Rail"**

- **Base Ecosystem Incubation**: Joining the accelerator to refine the product-market fit.
- **Secure Funding**: Securing initial grants and seed investment.
- **Regulatory Framework**: Filing for the **OJK Regulatory Sandbox** to ensure legal compliance for crypto-philanthropy in Indonesia.

## Q2 2026: Security & Pilot

**"Proving the Model"**

- **Smart Contract Audits**: Conducting professional security audits of the `CampaignVault` and `Donation` contracts.
- **IDRX QRIS Integration**: Finalizing the "Fiat-to-Onchain" gateway API.
- **Pilot Launch**: Launching the **Yogyakarta University Pilot**, targeting student hubs to test the gamification loop.

## Q3 2026: Launch & Growth

**"Opening the Floodgates"**

- **Mainnet Deployment**: Full protocol launch on Base Mainnet.
- **Genesis NFT Marketplace**: Activating the marketplace for "Impact Pet" skins and accessories.
- **Viral Integrations**: Deploying **Farcaster Frames** to allow donations directly within social feeds, maximizing viral sharing.

## Q4 2026: Scaling & TVL

**"Going National"**

- **Native Mobile Apps**: Releasing dedicated iOS/Android apps for mass adoption.
- **NGO SDK**: Deploying a plug-and-play Integration SDK for large NGOs to adopt the protocol.
- **National Expansion**: Launching a "Grassroots Roadshow" to expand beyond student hubs to national charities.
